About rEVe

Our mission to revolutionize EV charging

Who We Are

rEVe was founded in 2025 with a vision to create a sustainable future for electric vehicle charging. We believe that the transition to electric mobility should be accessible, affordable, and environmentally friendly.

Our hybrid charging solutions combine solar power with smart grid technology to provide the most efficient charging experience while reducing the carbon footprint of electric vehicles.

With a growing network of home and public charging stations, we're making EV charging convenient for everyone, everywhere.
